Deandra Neretlis: A Creator

An actress, writer, and a deep lover of fantasy. First drawn to the theatre stage, this recent graduate from Seneca's Acting for Camera and Voice program found herself drawn to acting of all kinds. Studying classic play acting, film acting, and even a little voice acting, she discovered the depths to which actors must go to develop characters and seeks to explore these ideas both through more experience and writing works of her own.
